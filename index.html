What is OpenChoreo?

 

OpenChoreo is an internal developer platform that helps platform engineering teams streamline developer workflows, simplify complexity, and deliver secure, scalable Internal Developer Portals — without building everything from scratch.

 

Built on the Cloud Native Stack

 

OpenChoreo orchestrates CNCF and open-source tools like Kubernetes, Argo CD, Cilium, Backstage, and more to provide a production-grade IDP.

 

What You Get with OpenChoreo

 

OpenChoreo isn’t just a toolkit—it’s a full foundation for building your Internal Developer Platform. It brings clarity, security, and self-service to every stage of your developer experience.

 

Golden Paths by Design

Define opinionated workflows that guide developers from idea to production—without needing to write pipelines from scratc.

Benefit:

Accelerates onboarding and delivery by reducing decision fatigue with clear, pre-approved workflows.

Impact:

Faster time-to-production, higher velocity, and more consistent, reliable releases.

Zero Trust Security Built In

Every connection and endpoint is explicitly declared and enforced via mTLS, Cilium, and fine-grained policies—no implicit access, ever.

Benefit:

Fine-grained access control, encryption, and security baked in from the start.

Impact:

Fewer security incidents, reduced blast radius, and smoother compliance with frameworks like SOC2 and ISO

Observable by Default

Logs, metrics, and traces are captured automatically—no extra setup needed.

Benefit:

Instant system insights and consistent observability across services.

Impact:

Faster debugging, reduced downtime, and improved Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR).

Built-in API Management

Ingress and egress are automated based on endpoint visibility. APIs are routed through gateways with built-in auth, rate limits, and traffic policies.

Benefit:

Eliminates manual routing and ensures secure, policy-based API access.

Impact:

Streamlined API exposure and consumption across internal and external consumers.

Composable and Extensible

Use just what you need. Integrates cleanly into your stack and supports GitOps and evolving strategies.

Benefit:

Modular by design—easy to adopt and integrate with existing tools.

Impact:

Faster adoption, less resistance to change, and long-term platform flexibility.

Clear Separation of Concerns

Platform engineers define the rules. Developers build features within safe, defined boundaries.

Benefit:

Reduces complexity for developers and increases clarity of roles.

Impact:

Improved productivity, fewer handoffs, and aligned team workflows.

Service Catalog and Discoverability

Every API, event, and data stream is registered and searchable.

Benefit:

Centralized discovery enables reuse and enforces governance.

Impact:

Faster development, reduced duplication, and stronger compliance.
